# Copywriting

## Purpose
Writes marketing copy that converts — homepages, landing pages, pricing pages, feature pages, and product descriptions. Uses proven frameworks (PAS, AIDA, FAB) and respects the reader's attention.

## When to use
- Writing or rewriting a homepage
- Building copy for a landing page or feature page
- Improving a pricing page that isn't converting
- Writing product descriptions for an ecommerce site
- Drafting headlines and subheads for an ad campaign

## Approach
- Lead with the customer's pain or desire, not the product
- Make every sentence earn the next
- Use specific numbers, names, and outcomes — avoid vague claims
- Prefer plain language over clever wordplay
- Close with a single, unambiguous call to action
