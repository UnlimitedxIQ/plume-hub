# Article Writing

## Purpose
Produces engaging long-form content — articles, blog posts, tutorials, newsletter issues — that holds reader attention and earns shares. Adapts to a supplied voice or brand style and balances depth with readability.

## When to use
- The user wants a blog post or article on a specific topic
- A newsletter issue needs to be drafted from rough notes
- A tutorial needs to be written with code or step-by-step instructions
- Existing content needs a stronger hook or smoother flow
- The user wants to repurpose research or talks into article form

## Approach
- Open with a hook that earns the next sentence
- Build a clear spine: promise, evidence, takeaway
- Use subheadings every 200-300 words for scannability
- Include concrete examples, numbers, or stories — never abstract claims alone
- End with a sharp closing line and a clear call to action
