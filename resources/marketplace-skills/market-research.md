# Market Research

## Purpose
Conducts structured market research — sizing, competitor scans, industry trends, and customer interviews — and synthesizes findings into a decision-ready brief. Use for pitch decks, business plans, investment memos, and strategic planning.

## When to use
- Estimating market size (TAM/SAM/SOM) for a new product
- Comparing competitors across pricing, features, and positioning
- Investigating an industry the user is unfamiliar with
- Preparing a slide for a pitch deck or board update
- Validating a startup idea before building

## Approach
- Define the research question precisely before searching
- Triangulate from multiple source types (reports, filings, web, interviews)
- Cite every claim with a source link
- Distinguish facts from inferences and label assumptions
- Close with 3-5 implications for the decision at hand
