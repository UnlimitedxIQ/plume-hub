# First Customers

## Purpose
Helps founders land their first paying customers without paid ads or a sales team. Based on the concentric-circles approach: friends and family, then community, then strangers. Practical and outreach-heavy.

## When to use
- The user has just launched and has zero customers
- A waitlist exists but isn't converting to revenue
- Cold outreach has stalled and needs a fresh angle
- The user is afraid of "selling" and needs scripts
- A founder needs to validate willingness to pay

## Approach
- Map concentric circles: warm network, communities, cold prospects
- Reach out personally to circle 1 — no automation
- Document objections and improve the pitch each round
- Move to circle 2 only after circle 1 is exhausted
- Track every conversation in a simple CRM (sheet is fine)
