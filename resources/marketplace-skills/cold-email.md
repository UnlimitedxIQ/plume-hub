# Cold Email

## Purpose
Writes effective B2B cold outreach — initial emails, follow-ups, and full sequences — that earn replies without crossing into spam. Tuned for founders, SDRs, and anyone selling without an existing audience.

## When to use
- Writing a first cold email to a target prospect
- Building a 4-7 step follow-up sequence
- Improving a cold email with poor reply rates
- Writing cold outreach for a partnership or feature ask
- Personalizing at scale without sounding like a template

## Approach
- Open with a specific, researched reason for emailing this person
- Keep the email under 100 words — every sentence earns the next
- Make the ask small and concrete
- Follow up 4-6 times with varied angles, never just "bumping this up"
- A/B test subject lines and openers based on actual reply rates
