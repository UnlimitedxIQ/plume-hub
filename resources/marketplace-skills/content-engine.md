# Content Engine

## Purpose
Designs and operates a multi-platform content system — creating native content for X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, and newsletters, then repurposing one source asset across channels without losing platform fit.

## When to use
- Building a content calendar for a personal or company brand
- Repurposing a podcast, talk, or article across platforms
- Writing platform-native posts that don't feel cross-posted
- Planning a launch campaign across multiple channels
- Establishing a sustainable content cadence

## Approach
- Create one source asset (talk, essay, podcast) per cycle
- Repurpose into 5-10 platform-native pieces — never just cross-post
- Match the medium: X = thread, LinkedIn = narrative, TikTok = hook
- Schedule consistently; consistency beats virality
- Track what works and double down on the format that lands
