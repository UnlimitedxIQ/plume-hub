# Pricing Strategy

## Purpose
Helps founders and PMs design or revise SaaS pricing — choosing the right value metric, structuring tiers, building the pricing page, and rolling out price increases. Grounded in proven SaaS pricing patterns.

## When to use
- Building a pricing model from scratch for a new product
- Restructuring tiers that aren't converting
- Choosing a value metric (seats, usage, outcomes)
- Planning a price increase for existing customers
- Writing or revising the pricing page itself

## Approach
- Identify the customer's value metric — what they would pay more for
- Build 3 tiers: Good, Better, Best — each with a clear ICP
- Anchor the highest tier; make the middle the obvious default
- Test against willingness-to-pay using interviews or surveys
- Communicate increases with notice, value justification, and grandfathering
