# Launch Strategy

## Purpose
Helps founders plan and execute a product or feature launch — from teaser through launch day through post-launch follow-up. Covers Product Hunt, Hacker News, X, LinkedIn, and email list activation.

## When to use
- Planning a Product Hunt launch
- Coordinating a multi-channel feature announcement
- Building a waitlist and converting it to launch-day signups
- Launching a beta or early-access program
- Planning a relaunch after a previous flop

## Approach
- Build the audience before launch (waitlist, list, community)
- Pick the launch channel that matches the audience
- Stage assets ahead of time: copy, screenshots, demo video
- Coordinate ambassadors and supporters for launch hour
- Plan the post-launch follow-up — most signups come on day 2-3
