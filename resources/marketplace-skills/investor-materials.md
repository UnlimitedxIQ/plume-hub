# Investor Materials

## Purpose
Produces high-quality investor materials for fundraising — pitch decks, written memos, simple financial models, and accelerator (YC, Techstars) applications. Tuned for early-stage founders.

## When to use
- Preparing a seed or pre-seed pitch deck
- Writing an investor memo or one-pager
- Building a simple financial model for diligence
- Drafting a YC or accelerator application
- Crafting a follow-up email after an investor meeting

## Approach
- Start from the investor's question, not the founder's enthusiasm
- Build a tight 10-12 slide deck: problem, solution, market, traction, team, ask
- Keep the financial model simple and defensible
- Tell a single coherent story across deck, memo, and emails
- End every artifact with a clear ask
